Script2Bit

Automate your script into call sheet

Script2Bit is a software application for film makers and producers that automates movie production scheduling tasks from a screenplay that you upload into the application. Script2 breaks down the screenplay into individual components for use in creating call sheets, SMS text and alert messages, email notices and other important elements of producing and completing your film project.
